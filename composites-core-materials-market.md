Honeycomb cores offer high strength-to-weight ratios, enhancing structural integrity while minimizing weight, commonly used in aerospace and automotive sectors. Balsa wood, known for its natural lightweight properties, is often utilized in marine and sports equipment, providing a sustainable option for enhancing composite strength and durability.</p></p>
