<p><p>The electrode drying oven market consists of two primary types: fixed electrode drying ovens and portable electrode dryers. Fixed electrode drying ovens are designed for stationary use in laboratories or industrial settings, providing larger capacity and more consistent temperature control for processing electrodes. In contrast, portable electrode dryers are compact and lightweight, allowing for mobility and ease of use in various locations. Both types are crucial for removing moisture from electrodes to enhance their performance in applications such as batteries and fuel cells.</p></p>
