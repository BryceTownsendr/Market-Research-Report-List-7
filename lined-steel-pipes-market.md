<p><p>Lined steel pipes are designed to enhance corrosion resistance and chemical compatibility. PP lined pipes utilize polypropylene for protective lining, ideal for moderate chemical resistance. PTFE lined pipes employ polytetrafluoroethylene, offering superior resistance to aggressive chemicals and high temperatures. PVDF lined pipes use polyvinylidene fluoride, known for its durability and excellent chemical resistance in harsher environments. Other market offerings may include alternative lining materials such as rubber or specialty coatings, tailored for diverse industrial applications and specific chemical handling needs.</p></p>
