<p><strong>The Fluoroelastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FKM</li><li>FSR</li><li>FFKM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoroelastomers are specialized synthetic rubbers known for their chemical resistance and thermal stability, commonly classified into three types: FKM, FSR, and FFKM. FKM (Fluorocarbon elastomer) is widely used in automotive and chemical applications due to its excellent resistance to fuels and oils. FSR (Fluorosilicone rubber) combines silicone properties with fluoropolymer benefits, suitable for applications exposed to aviation fuels. FFKM (Perfluoroelastomer) offers the highest performance, maintaining flexibility and durability in extreme temperatures and harsh chemicals, making it ideal for critical sealing applications.</p></p>
