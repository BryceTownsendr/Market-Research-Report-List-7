<p><p>Sludge dewatering equipment includes various types designed to reduce water content in sludge, enhancing disposal and treatment efficiency. </p><p>1. **Screw Press**: Utilizes a rotating screw to compress sludge, separating water with high efficiency.</p><p>2. **Rotary Disc Press**: Employs rotating discs to create pressure for liquid extraction.</p><p>3. **Centrifuges**: Uses centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids rapidly.</p><p>4. **Belt Filter Press**: Involves two belts that squeeze sludge between them, removing moisture.</p><p>5. **Other**: Encompasses alternative technologies and innovations addressing specific dewatering needs.</p></p>
