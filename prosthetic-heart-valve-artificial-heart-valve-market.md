Tissue heart valves, made from biological materials, offer a natural function and reduced clot risk but have a shorter lifespan. Mechanical heart valves, composed of durable materials, provide long-lasting solutions but require lifelong anticoagulation therapy. Each type serves distinct patient needs based on health conditions and lifestyle factors.</p></p>
