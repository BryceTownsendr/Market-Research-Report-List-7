<p><p>The multi-frequency GNSS receiver boards market is categorized into three main types: dual-frequency, tri-frequency, and others. Dual-frequency receivers operate on two GNSS signals, enhancing accuracy and mitigating errors caused by ionospheric delays. Tri-frequency receivers utilize three signals, providing even greater precision and reliability, particularly in challenging environments. The "others" category encompasses specialized receivers that may use additional frequencies or specific configurations for unique applications, catering to specialized industries such as aviation, agriculture, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
