<p><p>Single-ply roofing encompasses three key types: PVC, EPDM, and TPO. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is known for its durability, excellent resistance to UV rays, and chlorine, making it ideal for commercial installations. EPDM (rubber) offers exceptional weather resistance and flexibility, suitable for various climates and easy to install. TPO (thermoplastic) combines the benefits of both PVC and EPDM, featuring reflective properties for energy efficiency and a seamless installation process. Each type caters to specific needs in roofing applications.</p></p>
