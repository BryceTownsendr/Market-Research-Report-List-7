<p><p>The linoleum market comprises three primary types: sheet linoleum flooring, linoleum tile flooring, and floating linoleum floors. Sheet linoleum flooring consists of large, continuous rolls that offer a seamless appearance, ideal for areas requiring robust, moisture-resistant surfaces. Linoleum tile flooring features individual tiles that allow for customizable designs and easier repair. Floating linoleum floors are not adhered to the subfloor, providing flexibility and ease of installation, making them suitable for various residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
