TBE buffer is favored for DNA fragment analysis due to its superior resolution and stability, making it ideal for long runs. TAE buffer is commonly used for agarose gel electrophoresis as it offers better DNA recovery and is less toxic. Other buffers may include variations like phosphate or custom formulations tailored for specific applications, expanding versatility in molecular biology techniques.</p></p>
