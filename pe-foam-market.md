<p><strong>The PE Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IXPE</li><li>XPE</li><li>EPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE foam market encompasses three key types: IXPE, XPE, and EPE. IXPE (Irradiated Cross-Linked Polyethylene) offers superior durability and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for high-performance applications. XPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) is less expensive and provides good thermal insulation, commonly used in packaging and construction. EPE (Expanded Polyethylene) is lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective, making it popular for protective packaging. Each type serves distinct industries, leveraging their unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
