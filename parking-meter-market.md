<p><p>The parking meter market consists of two primary types: single space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single space meters are individual units that manage parking for one vehicle each, facilitating easy monitoring and collection of fees. In contrast, multi-space kiosks serve multiple parking spaces, allowing users to pay for parking from a central location. These kiosks enhance efficiency by processing payments for several spots at once, reducing the number of devices needed and streamlining parking management in urban areas.</p></p>
