<p><strong>The Whey Protein Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Hydrolysate Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whey protein powder market is segmented into three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), and Whey Protein Hydrolysate (WPH). WPC contains 70-80% protein, retaining beneficial nutrients but with higher fat and lactose content, making it ideal for general fitness. WPI is more refined, offering over 90% protein with minimal fat and lactose, catering to those with lactose sensitivities or higher protein needs. WPH undergoes pre-digestion for faster absorption, often used in medical protein supplementation and sports nutrition.</p></p>
