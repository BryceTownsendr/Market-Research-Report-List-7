<p><strong>The Optically Clear Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Capacitance Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optically Clear Adhesive (OCA) market comprises primarily two types: resistance type and capacitance type. Resistance type OCA is primarily used for bonding displays and touch panels, providing durability and optical clarity, often in applications where touch sensitivity is less critical. In contrast, capacitance type OCA is designed for touch-sensitive applications, enabling precise touch response and screen performance. Both types enhance display quality, but their applications diverge based on the technological requirements of the devices they serve.</p></p>
