<p><p>Pressure isolation valves are crucial in managing fluid flow within various systems, categorized into several types. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with minimal flow restriction. Butterfly valves are lightweight and compact, ideal for large volume applications. Knife gate valves excel in handling thick liquids and provide tight sealing. Plug valves allow for smooth, quarter-turn operation, effectively isolating flow. Other types may include check valves and globe valves, each designed for specific applications to ensure safety and efficiency in fluid systems.</p></p>
