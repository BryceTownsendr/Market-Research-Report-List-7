<p><p>Smartphone display panels are primarily categorized into four types: LCD, IPS-LCD, OLED, and AMOLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) relies on backlighting, offering vibrant colors and good brightness. IPS-LCD (In-Plane Switching) enhances viewing angles and color accuracy, making it popular in mid-range devices. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) allows for deeper blacks and higher contrast by illuminating pixels individually. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) further optimizes performance, providing better response times and energy efficiency, making it the preferred choice for premium smartphones.</p></p>
