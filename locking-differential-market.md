<p><ul><li>Automatic Locking Differential</li><li>Selectable Locking Differential</li><li>Spool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A **locking differential** ensures both wheels on an axle rotate at the same speed, improving traction in off-road or slippery conditions. The **automatic locking differential** engages automatically when wheel slip is detected, providing seamless traction without driver input. The **selectable locking differential** allows the driver to manually engage or disengage the lock as needed, offering flexibility for varying terrains. The **spool market** refers to solid axle systems that permanently lock both wheels together, ideal for high-performance or racing applications where constant traction is critical.</p></p>
