<p><p>The electronic assembly materials market comprises various types designed for specific applications in electronics manufacturing. Adhesives are used for bonding components to substrates, providing mechanical strength. Paste fluxes facilitate soldering by reducing oxidation and improving wetting during the assembly process. Electrically conductive materials, such as conductive adhesives and inks, ensure electrical connections between components. Thermal interface materials enhance heat dissipation in electronic devices, improving performance and reliability. Collectively, these materials are essential for efficient and durable electronic assembly.</p></p>
