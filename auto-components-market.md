Pressure plates engage and disengage the clutch, enabling gear shifts. Clutches connect and disconnect the engine from the transmission, allowing controlled power transfer. Other components include bearings, gears, and housings, supporting overall vehicle performance. These parts are critical for manual and automatic transmissions, ensuring efficiency, durability, and seamless driving experiences across various automotive applications.</p></p>
