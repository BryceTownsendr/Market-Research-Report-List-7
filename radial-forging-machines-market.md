<p><strong>The Radial Forging Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Forging</li><li>Cold Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial forging machines are utilized in two primary market types: hot forging and cold forging. Hot forging involves shaping metal at elevated temperatures, allowing for improved ductility and reduced material hardness, making it easier to form complex shapes. In contrast, cold forging takes place at room temperature, resulting in enhanced strength and tighter tolerances due to work hardening. Both processes are integral in producing high-quality forged components for various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
