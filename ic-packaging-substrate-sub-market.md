<p><p>The IC packaging substrate market comprises three main types: organic substrates, inorganic substrates, and composite substrates. Organic substrates are primarily made from materials like epoxy resin and are widely used for their flexibility and electrical performance. Inorganic substrates, often made from ceramics or glass, provide excellent thermal stability and are utilized in high-performance applications. Composite substrates combine organic and inorganic materials to leverage the benefits of both, offering enhanced performance characteristics for advanced electronic devices in various applications.</p></p>
