<p><p>The Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) 3D printer market features two primary powder feeding types: coaxial powder feeding and roller powder feeding. Coaxial powder feeding delivers metal powder directly through a nozzle, ensuring precise layering and reduced wastage, ideal for complex geometries. In contrast, roller powder feeding employs a roller mechanism to spread a uniform layer of powder across the build platform, enhancing speed and efficiency for larger prints. Both methods cater to various industrial applications and production needs.</p></p>
