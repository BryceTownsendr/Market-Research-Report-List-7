<p><p>The Liquid Flowmeter market is categorized into stationary and portable liquid flow meters. Stationary liquid flow meters are fixed installations used in industrial settings for continuous monitoring and measurement of liquid flow rates in pipelines. They are designed for accuracy and reliability in high-volume applications.
