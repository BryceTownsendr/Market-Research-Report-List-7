<p><p>The Circular Dichroism (CD) spectrometers market is categorized by the type of light sources used: Linearly Polarized Light Sources, Circularly Polarized Light Sources, and Multiple Light Sources. Linearly polarized light sources utilize light that vibrates in a single plane, essentially serving as a base mechanism. Multiple light sources combine both linear and circular polarization, offering versatile options for advanced spectroscopic analysis of biomolecules and materials.</p></p>
