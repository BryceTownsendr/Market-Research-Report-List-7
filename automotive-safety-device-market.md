<p><p>The **Automotive Safety Device Market** comprises **Active Safety Systems** and **Passive Safety Systems**. **Active Safety Systems** focus on preventing accidents by enhancing driver control and vehicle stability, including features like Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS). **Passive Safety Systems** aim to minimize injury during accidents, such as airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both systems work together to improve road safety, with active systems preventing collisions and passive systems protecting occupants when accidents occur.</p></p>
