<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Tailgate</li><li>Thermoset Tailgate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic tailgate market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermoset tailgates. Thermoplastic tailgates are made from materials that can be re-melted and reshaped, offering flexibility, lightweight properties, and ease of recycling, which enhances fuel efficiency. In contrast, thermoset tailgates are composed of materials that undergo a chemical change when cured, providing superior strength, heat resistance, and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs and design preferences.</p></p>
