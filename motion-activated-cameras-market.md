Conversely, wired motion-activated cameras connect through cables, providing stable power and data transfer, which may enhance reliability and reduce interference. Each type caters to different user preferences, with wireless options ideal for easy installation and mobility, while wired cameras suit applications requiring consistent, uninterrupted service.</p></p>
