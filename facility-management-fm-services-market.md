<p><p>Facility Management (FM) services are categorized into **Soft Services** and **Hard Services**. **Soft Services** focus on enhancing occupant comfort and well-being, including cleaning, landscaping, security, waste management, and pest control. These are non-technical but essential for maintaining a pleasant environment. **Hard Services** involve technical and structural maintenance, such as HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical systems, fire safety, and building automation.
