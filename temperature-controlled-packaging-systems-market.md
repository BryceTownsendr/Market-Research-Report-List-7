<p><strong>The Temperature Controlled Packaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Systems</li><li>Passive Systems</li><li>Hybrid Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature controlled packaging systems are vital for transporting sensitive products, primarily pharmaceuticals and food. Active systems utilize external power sources, such as refrigeration or heating units, to maintain specific temperature ranges. Passive systems rely on insulated materials and refrigerants without external power, providing a cost-effective solution for moderate temperature control. Hybrid systems combine both active and passive elements to optimize temperature management, offering flexibility and enhanced performance for varying logistical needs. These systems ensure product integrity during transportation.</p></p>
