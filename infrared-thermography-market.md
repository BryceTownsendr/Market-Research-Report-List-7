<p><p>The Infrared Thermography market comprises various types, notably laser and structured light systems. Laser thermography utilizes high-intensity lasers to detect temperature variations accurately, enabling detailed thermal imaging for applications like industrial inspections and biomedical diagnostics. Structured light thermography employs projected patterns of light to capture surface temperature distribution, enhancing 3D measurement capabilities. Both technologies serve diverse sectors, including manufacturing, aerospace, and healthcare, by facilitating non-destructive testing, quality control, and process monitoring through effective thermal analysis.</p></p>
