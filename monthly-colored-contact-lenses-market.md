<p><p>The monthly colored contact lenses market is primarily divided into two types: hydrogel and silicone hydrogel. Hydrogel lenses are made from a water-containing polymer, offering comfort and moisture but may not provide the best oxygen permeability. In contrast, silicone hydrogel lenses are engineered for enhanced oxygen flow to the eye, making them suitable for extended wear and reducing the risk of dryness and irritation. Both types cater to consumers seeking aesthetic enhancement while ensuring varying levels of comfort and performance.</p></p>
