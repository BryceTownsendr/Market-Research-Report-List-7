<p><p>The ECMO First Aid Equipment market is categorized primarily into two types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is designed to support patients with severe cardiac and respiratory failure by providing both oxygenation and circulatory support. In contrast, VV ECMO primarily aids patients suffering from respiratory failure by oxygenating blood while allowing for normal heart function. Both types are critical in emergency settings, enhancing patient survival rates during life-threatening conditions.</p></p>
