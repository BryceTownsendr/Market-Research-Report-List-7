<p><p>The visible laser diode market can be categorized into three main types: continuous, pulsed, and other variants. Continuous laser diodes emit a steady beam of light, ideal for applications such as laser printing and medical devices. Pulsed laser diodes generate short bursts of light, suitable for range finding and communication systems. The "other" category includes specialized types, such as modulated or tunable diodes, which cater to niche applications in research, military, and industrial settings.</p></p>
