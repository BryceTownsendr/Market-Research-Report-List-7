<p><p>The Fluid-Applied Concrete Waterproofing Membrane market primarily consists of single-component and two-component types. Single-component membranes are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture or air, offering ease of application and quick drying. In contrast, two-component membranes require a blending of a resin with a hardener before application, providing enhanced durability and resistance to various environmental factors. Both types are essential for waterproofing in construction, but their choice depends on specific project requirements and desired performance levels.</p></p>
