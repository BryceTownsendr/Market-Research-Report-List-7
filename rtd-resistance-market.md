<p><strong>The RTD Resistance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-wire RTD</li><li>Three-wire RTD</li><li>Four-wire RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTD resistance market includes three main types: Two-wire, Three-wire, and Four-wire RTDs. Two-wire RTDs are simple and cost-effective but susceptible to error from lead resistance. Three-wire RTDs improve accuracy by compensating for lead resistance, making them suitable for many applications. Four-wire RTDs offer the highest precision by completely eliminating lead resistance effects, ideal for critical measurements. Each type caters to different applications and needs, influencing their market demand based on accuracy, cost, and complexity.</p></p>
