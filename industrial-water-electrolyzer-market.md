<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer</li><li>PEM Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial water electrolyzer market features two primary types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte to facilitate hydrogen production, offering cost-effectiveness and reliability for large-scale applications. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency, compact design, and faster response times, making them ideal for dynamic applications and integration with renewable energy sources. Both types play crucial roles in advancing hydrogen solutions for various industries.</p></p>
