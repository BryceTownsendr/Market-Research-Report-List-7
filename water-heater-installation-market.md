<p><ul><li>Tank Storage</li><li>Tankless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water heater installation market is primarily divided into two types: tank storage and tankless systems. Tank storage water heaters maintain a reservoir of heated water, providing an immediate supply but requiring significant space and energy for continuous heating. In contrast, tankless water heaters heat water on demand, offering energy efficiency and compact design. While tank storage systems are often chosen for their reliability and lower upfront costs, tankless models appeal to those seeking long-term savings and reduced energy consumption.</p></p>
