<p><p>The commercial spinning bike market features two main types: belt drive and chain drive bikes. Belt drive spinning bikes use a rubber or polyurethane belt for a smoother, quieter ride with less maintenance, making them suitable for high-frequency usage in gyms. Conversely, chain drive spinning bikes utilize a metal chain, offering a more authentic cycling feel and robustness, often preferred by performance-focused riders. Each type caters to different preferences, balancing performance, durability, and user experience in fitness settings.</p></p>
