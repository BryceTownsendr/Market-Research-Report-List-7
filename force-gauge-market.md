<p><p>The force gauge market is primarily divided into two types: digital and mechanical force gauges. Digital force gauges utilize electronic sensors to provide precise and easily readable measurements, often featuring data logging capabilities and user-friendly interfaces, making them ideal for complex applications. Mechanical force gauges, on the other hand, rely on analog mechanisms to measure force, offering simplicity and durability, but may lack the precision of their digital counterparts. Both types serve various industrial and laboratory applications depending on user needs.</p></p>
