<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Road Simulation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle-Coupled Road Simulators</li><li>Tire-Coupled Road Simulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road simulation systems are used to replicate real-world driving conditions for vehicle testing. Spindle-coupled road simulators directly connect to a vehicle's suspension system, applying forces to the wheels to simulate road surfaces, offering precise control for durability and performance testing. Tire-coupled road simulators use rollers to interact with the vehicle's tires, mimicking road conditions for dynamic testing, including handling and braking. Both systems are essential for automotive R&D, ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
