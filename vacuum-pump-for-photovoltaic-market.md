<p><p>The Vacuum Pump for Photovoltaic Market includes three primary types: Screw Vacuum Pumps, Roots Vacuum Pumps, and others. Screw vacuum pumps utilize a pair of helical screws to compress and move gases, offering high efficiency and reliability for solar panel manufacturing. Roots vacuum pumps use two rotating lobes to create a vacuum, ideal for high-flow applications. Other types may include diaphragm and rotary vane pumps, each serving specific needs in the photovoltaic production process, such as material handling and system maintenance.</p></p>
