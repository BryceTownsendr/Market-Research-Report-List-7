<p><p>The sulfur-containing silane market is categorized into three primary types: polysulfide, mercapto, and others. Polysulfide silanes are used for their flexibility and adhesion properties in sealants and coatings. Mercapto silanes, characterized by the presence of thiol groups, enhance adhesion and durability, particularly in rubber and polymer applications. Other types encompass various specialized silanes that provide unique functionalities for niche applications across industries like construction, automotive, and electronics, catering to diverse needs in surface modification and bonding technologies.</p></p>
