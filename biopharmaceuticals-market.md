<p><p>The biopharmaceutical market encompasses various types, each with distinct therapeutic applications. Monoclonal antibodies target specific cells for cancer and autoimmune diseases. Interferons modulate immune responses, useful in viral infections and cancers. Colony-stimulating factors stimulate blood cell production, aiding patients undergoing chemotherapy. Erythropoietin boosts red blood cell formation, treating anemia. Insulin regulates blood sugar in diabetes, while vaccines provide immunity against infectious diseases. Growth hormones stimulate growth and metabolism, primarily for hormone deficiencies. The "Others" category includes additional biological products and therapies.</p></p>
