<p><p>The advanced wound management products market encompasses three primary types: advanced wound dressings, therapy devices, and active wound care products. Advanced wound dressings include specialized materials that promote healing and protect wounds from infection. Therapy devices consist of tools like negative pressure wound therapy systems that enhance healing through suction methods. Active wound care products incorporate biological agents or growth factors to stimulate tissue regeneration and enhance healing, addressing complex and chronic wounds more effectively than traditional methods.</p></p>
