<p><p>The aluminum die cut lids market consists primarily of two types: heat seal and sealant seal. Heat seal lids are designed to bond securely to containers through the application of heat, creating an airtight seal that preserves product freshness. Sealant seal lids use adhesive coatings to adhere to container tops, providing effective sealing without requiring additional heat. Both types are widely used in packaging for food, beverages, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring product integrity and extending shelf life.</p></p>
