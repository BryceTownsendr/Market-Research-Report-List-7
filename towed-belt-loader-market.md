<p><p>The towed belt loader market consists of various transmission types catering to diverse operational needs. Hydraulic-mechanical transmission combines hydraulic power and mechanical elements, allowing for efficient load handling with versatile control. Hydraulic transmission relies solely on hydraulic systems for smooth operation, offering high reliability for heavy loads. Power transmission utilizes traditional mechanical methods, ensuring robust performance but may lack the finesse of hydraulic systems. Each type serves distinct applications in airport ground support, logistics, and material handling industries.</p></p>
