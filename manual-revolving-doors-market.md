<p><p>The manual revolving doors market is categorized into two main types: three wings type and four wings type. Three-wing revolving doors feature three equally spaced panels, offering efficient space usage and lower wind resistance, making them suitable for moderate traffic areas. In contrast, four-wing revolving doors consist of four panels, allowing for higher traffic volume and enhanced climate control, as they create a tighter seal against external elements. Both designs enhance aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency in commercial spaces.</p></p>
