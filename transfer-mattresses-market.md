<p><p>The transfer mattresses market is categorized into three main types: Dynamic Air, Foam, and Vacuum mattresses. Dynamic Air mattresses utilize air-filled cells that can be adjusted to provide varying levels of support and pressure relief, promoting patient comfort and reducing the risk of pressure ulcers. Foam mattresses are made from high-density materials offering superior cushioning and stability, while Vacuum mattresses are designed to be molded around the patient, providing custom support and immobilization for safe transportation in medical settings.</p></p>
