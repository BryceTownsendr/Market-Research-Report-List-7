<p><p>The polyurethane elastic sealant market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component sealants. One-component polyurethane elastic sealant is ready to use without requiring any mixing, offering ease of application and quick curing properties. Conversely, two-component polyurethane elastic sealant involves mixing a resin and a hardener before application, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing for their strong adhesive properties and flexibility, making them suitable for various sealing applications.</p></p>
