<p><p>The stainless steel buffer tank market is primarily divided into two types: stainless steel chilled buffer tanks and stainless steel thermal water buffer tanks. Chilled buffer tanks are designed for cooling applications, effectively storing chilled water to balance demand fluctuations in HVAC systems. In contrast, thermal water buffer tanks are used to store hot water, improving energy efficiency in heating systems by minimizing cycling of boilers and ensuring a steady supply of hot water. Both types enhance system performance and longevity.</p></p>
