<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>Stainless Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor check valves market primarily features two key types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and stainless steel. PTFE check valves are known for their chemical resistance and low friction, making them ideal for highly corrosive environments in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, stainless steel check valves offer durability and high-temperature stability, suitable for applications requiring robust physical properties. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining fluid flow, preventing backflow, and ensuring process integrity in semiconductor production environments.</p></p>
