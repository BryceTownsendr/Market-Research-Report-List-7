<p><p>The sustainable high-barrier paper market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided coatings. Single-sided coating involves applying a barrier layer on one side of the paper, making it suitable for specific applications like packaging or labels where only one side needs protection from moisture and gases. Conversely, double-sided coating offers enhanced protection on both sides, ideal for applications requiring superior barrier properties, such as food packaging and pharmaceutical products. Both types aim to provide eco-friendly solutions while maintaining functionality.</p></p>
