<p><p>The High Density Fiberboard (HDF) panel market consists of three main types: General HDF, Moisture Resistant HDF, and Fire-Rated HDF. General HDF is widely used for furniture and flooring due to its smooth surface and cost-effectiveness. Moisture Resistant HDF is designed for areas exposed to humidity, making it suitable for kitchens and bathrooms. Fire-Rated HDF offers enhanced safety features by resisting ignition and slowing down fire spread, making it ideal for commercial applications where fire safety is a concern.</p></p>
