<p><p>The magnetron sputtering system market comprises two primary types: Permanent Magnet Type and Electromagnetic Type. Permanent Magnet Type utilizes fixed magnets to create a magnetic field, enhancing target material deposition efficiency and uniformity, making it ideal for various thin-film applications. In contrast, Electromagnetic Type employs electromagnets which can be adjusted for customizable performance, allowing for flexibility in target material and process control. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as electronics, optics, and surface coatings, shaping advanced materials development.</p></p>
