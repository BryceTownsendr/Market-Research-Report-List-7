<p><p>The industrial garnet market encompasses several types, each with unique properties and applications. Almandine garnet is primarily used for abrasive materials due to its hardness. Andradite garnet, known for its high refractive index, is often utilized in decorative stones and industry-specific applications. Grossular garnet is valued in the gem industry and for filtration purposes. Pyrope garnet is popular in abrasives and gemstones, while Spessartine garnet is used in specialized industrial applications. Uvarovite garnet primarily finds use in niche jewelry markets.</p></p>
