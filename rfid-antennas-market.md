<p><strong>The RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Antennas</li><li>Passive RFID Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID antennas market is divided into two main types: active and passive RFID antennas. Active RFID antennas are used in systems where tags are powered by batteries, allowing for greater transmission range and real-time tracking capabilities. In contrast, passive RFID antennas rely on energy emitted by RFID readers, making them more cost-effective and suitable for applications like inventory management, where proximity and short-range communication are sufficient. Both types serve distinct purposes across various industries, enhancing tracking and data collection processes.</p></p>
