<p><p>The automotive bicycle rack market features various types tailored to different needs. Hitch-mounted racks attach to the vehicle's hitch, allowing easy loading and unloading while providing stability for multiple bikes. Roof-mounted racks require lifting bikes to the roof but maximize rear visibility. Trunk-mounted racks are simple to install, securing bikes on the trunk, ideal for occasional use. Pickup carriers are designed for pickup trucks, allowing secure bike transport in the truck bed without sacrificing cargo space. Each type offers unique advantages based on user preferences and vehicle compatibility.</p></p>
