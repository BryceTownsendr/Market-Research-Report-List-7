<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) material market comprises two key segments: CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive materials designed for polishing semiconductor wafers, ensuring a smooth, flat surface during manufacturing. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are chemical mixtures that aid in the removal of material while simultaneously providing polishing action. Both components are essential for achieving precise surface finishes in semiconductor production, playing a critical role in the fabrication of integrated circuits and advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
