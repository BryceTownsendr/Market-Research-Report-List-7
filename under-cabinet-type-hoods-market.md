<p><strong>The Under Cabinet Type Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Hood</li><li>Centrifugal Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under cabinet type hoods are essential in kitchen ventilation, primarily categorized into axial flow and centrifugal hoods. Axial flow hoods use rotating blades to move air parallel to the blade axis, offering efficient airflow for smaller kitchens. They are generally quieter and less complex. Centrifugal hoods, on the other hand, leverage a fan to draw air in and expel it at right angles, providing higher air pressure and efficiency, suitable for larger spaces with higher cooking demands. Both types enhance indoor air quality.</p></p>
