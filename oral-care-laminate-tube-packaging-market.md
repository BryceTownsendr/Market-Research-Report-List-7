<p><p>The Oral Care Laminate Tube Packaging market is primarily categorized into two types: ABL (Aluminium Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes offer superior moisture and oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for preserving the freshness of toothpaste and oral gels. In contrast, PBL tubes are more flexible and lightweight, often providing cost-effective solutions with decent protection. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and packaging requirements, influencing their usage in oral care products globally.</p></p>
