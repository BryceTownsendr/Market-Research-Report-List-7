<p><p>The plaque disclosing aids market comprises primarily two types: plaque disclosing gels and plaque disclosing tablets. Plaque disclosing gels are typically viscous substances applied with a brush, highlighting plaque accumulation on teeth through staining. These gels enable effective visualization for both dental professionals and patients. Conversely, plaque disclosing tablets are chewable indicators that, when dissolved, reveal plaque presence through coloration on the teeth. Both types aim to enhance oral hygiene awareness and assist in plaque removal, promoting better dental health.</p></p>
