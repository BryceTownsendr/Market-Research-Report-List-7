<p><p>Industrial caliper brakes are categorized by product type into floating and fixed types. Floating caliper brakes allow for slight movement, enhancing braking efficiency and even wear, while fixed caliper brakes provide a rigid structure for consistent force application. Furthermore, they are classified by driven type into hydraulic and pneumatic systems. Hydraulic calipers use fluid pressure for force, offering precise control and high power, whereas pneumatic calipers rely on air pressure, providing rapid response and lightweight operation suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
