<p><p>The polyadipates market comprises three main types based on polymer backbone: polyethylene adipate (PEA), polypropylene adipate (PPA), and polybutylene adipate (PBA). PEA is known for its flexibility and biodegradability, making it suitable for applications in packaging and coatings. PPA offers enhanced thermal stability and is often used in automotive and industrial applications. PBA boasts superior elasticity and is ideal for producing versatile elastomers. Together, these types cater to various industries, including packaging, textiles, and automotive, driven by sustainability trends.</p></p>
