<p><p>The Jet Windscreen Wiper market consists of three main types: boneless, bone, and hybrid wipers. Boneless wipers feature a sleek design without a traditional metal frame, offering improved aerodynamics and quieter operation. Bone type wipers, on the other hand, utilize a rigid frame for better durability and performance in harsh weather conditions. Hybrid wipers combine the benefits of both types, featuring a metal structure with a flexible rubber blade, ensuring effective cleaning while reducing wind lift.</p></p>
