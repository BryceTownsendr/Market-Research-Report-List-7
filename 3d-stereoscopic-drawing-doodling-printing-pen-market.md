<p><p>The 3D Stereoscopic Drawing Doodling Printing Pen market can be categorized into two main types: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D printing pens and Stereolithography (SLA) 3D printing pens. FDM pens work by melting plastic filament to create three-dimensional shapes, allowing for easy manipulation and design. In contrast, SLA pens utilize ultraviolet light to cure liquid resin, resulting in highly detailed and smooth finishes. Each type caters to different user preferences, from casual doodlers to professional artists seeking precision.</p></p>
