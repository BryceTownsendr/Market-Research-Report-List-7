<p><strong>The Touch Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch sensors market is primarily segmented into resistive and capacitive types. Resistive touch sensors operate by sensing pressure applied to the screen, making them suitable for various environments, including those with moisture or dirt. In contrast, capacitive touch sensors detect changes in electrical capacitance when a finger touches the surface, enabling multi-touch capabilities and offering higher transparency and durability. Each type serves distinct applications, with resistive commonly used in industrial settings and capacitive in consumer electronics.</p></p>
