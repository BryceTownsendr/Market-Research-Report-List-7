<p><p>The aluminum alloy casting market is categorized into three main types: die casting, permanent mold casting, and others. </p><p>Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold to produce intricate shapes with precise dimensions, ideal for high-volume production. Permanent mold casting uses reusable molds to create durable parts and is suited for medium production runs with good surface finish. Other methods, like sand casting and investment casting, serve specific applications and offer flexibility in fabrication and material properties.</p></p>
