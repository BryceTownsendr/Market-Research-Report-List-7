<p><p>Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs) are mechanical devices that assist the heart in pumping blood, primarily used in critical care settings for patients with severe cardiac conditions. These devices work by inflating and deflating a balloon placed in the aorta, thereby enhancing myocardial oxygen perfusion and reducing cardiac workload. Their applications are widespread, including during high-risk cardiac surgeries and in managing acute coronary syndromes.</p><p>The Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
