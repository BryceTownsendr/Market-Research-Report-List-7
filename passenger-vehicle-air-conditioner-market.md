<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Air Conditioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle air conditioner market features two main types: reciprocating and rotary compressors. Reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress refrigerant, providing strong cooling but are generally bulkier and heavier. In contrast, rotary compressors operate using rotating elements, making them more compact, lighter, and quieter. Both types serve to regulate cabin temperature, but their efficiency, size, and design differences cater to varying consumer preferences and vehicle specifications, influencing choices in modern automotive HVAC systems.</p></p>
