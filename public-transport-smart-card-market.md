<p><p>The Public Transport Smart Card market consists of two primary types: contactless smart cards and contact smart cards. Contactless smart cards utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to tap their card on readers without physical contact, enhancing speed and convenience in fare collection. In contrast, contact smart cards require physical insertion into a reader for authentication and transaction processing. Both types serve to streamline public transport operations, improve user experience, and reduce boarding times for passengers.</p></p>
