<p><p>The two-way radio market can be categorized into analog and digital types. Analog radios transmit voice signals using continuous waveforms, offering straightforward communication but limited features and capacity. In contrast, digital radios convert voice into data, allowing for clearer audio, enhanced features like text messaging, encryption, and increased channel capacity. Digital systems efficiently use bandwidth and provide better range and versatility, making them increasingly popular across industries such as emergency services, construction, and event management.</p></p>
