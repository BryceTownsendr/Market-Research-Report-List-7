<p><ul><li>Internal Electroceutical Devices</li><li>External Electroceutical Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electroceutical devices market is categorized into internal and external devices. Internal electroceutical devices are implanted within the body, often targeting specific nerves or tissues to modulate physiological functions, offering treatment for conditions such as chronic pain, epilepsy, and depression. In contrast, external electroceutical devices operate outside the body, applying electrical stimulation through the skin to manage pain or improve muscle function. Both types utilize bioelectronic technology to enhance patient outcomes by harnessing the body's electrical signaling for therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
