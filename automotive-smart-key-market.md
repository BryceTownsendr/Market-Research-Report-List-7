<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Smart Key Market encompasses two main types: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically using a key, relying on proximity sensors to detect the key fob. In contrast, RKES requires users to press buttons on the key fob to unlock doors or start the engine, offering convenience without the need for traditional keys. Both systems enhance security and ease of vehicle access.</p></p>
