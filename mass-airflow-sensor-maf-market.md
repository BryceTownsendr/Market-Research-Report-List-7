<p><ul><li>Vane Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor</li><li>Hot Film Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF) market comprises various sensor types, each serving specific applications. Vane Air Flow Sensors utilize a mechanical vane to measure airflow, typically in older vehicles. Karman Vortex Sensors detect airflow based on vortex shedding patterns, offering high accuracy. Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors use a heated wire to measure airflow based on cooling effects, ideal for precise readings. Hot Film Air Flow Sensors function similarly but employ a thin film, ensuring rapid response and durability in modern engines.</p></p>
