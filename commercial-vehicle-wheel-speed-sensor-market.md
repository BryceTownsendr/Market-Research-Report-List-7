<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market includes Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors use the Hall effect to detect wheel speed, offering high accuracy, durability, and resistance to environmental factors. They are widely used in modern vehicles for precise speed measurement. Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on magnetic fields and induction to measure speed, providing cost-effective and reliable performance. Both types are essential for anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and traction control, ensuring vehicle safety and efficiency.</p></p>
