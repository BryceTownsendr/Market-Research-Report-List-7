<p><p>The Insertable Cardiac Recorder (ICR) market is divided into two main types: smartphone compatible and smartphone incompatible. Smartphone compatible ICRs integrate with mobile devices, allowing real-time data transfer and user-friendly monitoring through dedicated apps. This enhances patient engagement and enables remote access for healthcare providers. In contrast, smartphone incompatible ICRs function independently without mobile connectivity, relying on conventional data retrieval methods. While they offer robust monitoring, they lack the convenience and immediacy provided by smartphone integration in managing cardiac health.</p></p>
