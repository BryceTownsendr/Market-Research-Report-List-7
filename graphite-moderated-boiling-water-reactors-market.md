<p><p>Graphite Moderated Boiling Water Reactors (GMBWRs) utilize graphite to slow down neutrons, enhancing the nuclear fission process. The market features two main types: Single Cycle Steam Generation, where the reactor directly produces steam from boiling water in a single loop, optimizing efficiency for electricity generation. In contrast, Dual Cycle Steam Generation employs a two-loop system, with one loop transferring heat to generate steam, allowing for improved heat management and higher overall efficiency. Both types cater to distinct energy production needs.</p></p>
