<p><ul><li>Gantry Car Washing Systems</li><li>Conveyor Tunnel Washing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic car washing systems market is primarily divided into two types: gantry car washing systems and conveyor tunnel washing systems. Gantry systems utilize a fixed framework over the vehicle, moving brushes and nozzles to clean the car as it remains stationary. In contrast, conveyor tunnel systems involve a continuous belt that transports vehicles through a series of washing stages, ensuring a thorough clean. Both systems offer efficiency and convenience, catering to different consumer needs in car care.</p></p>
