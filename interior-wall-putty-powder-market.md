<p><p>The interior wall putty powder market is primarily categorized into two types: cement-based putty and gypsum-based putty. Cement-based putty is known for its durability and moisture resistance, making it ideal for exterior applications and humid environments. In contrast, gypsum-based putty offers a smoother finish and quicker drying time, making it suitable for interior spaces. Both types are used to create a smooth surface for painting and enhance the overall aesthetic of interior walls, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
