<p><p>The H-Bridge driver market is categorized into two main types: Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge. A Single H-Bridge is designed to control one DC motor or load, providing simple and cost-effective solutions for applications requiring unidirectional control. In contrast, a Dual H-Bridge can control two DC motors or loads independently, enabling bidirectional control and more complex functionalities. This differentiation affects their applications, with Single H-Bridges being common in simpler devices and Dual H-Bridges favored in robotics and automation systems.</p></p>
