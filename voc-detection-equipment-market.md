<p><strong>The VOC Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC detection equipment includes various technologies, primarily photoionization detectors (PID) and metal-oxide semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, providing real-time, accurate measurements of low-concentration gases, making them ideal for safety monitoring in industrial settings. Conversely, MOS sensors operate by changing resistance in response to gas exposure, offering a cost-effective solution for detecting a broader range of VOCs. Each technology has distinct advantages depending on application, sensitivity, and response time requirements in environmental monitoring.</p></p>
