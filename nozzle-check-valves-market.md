<p><strong>The Nozzle Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flanged End</li><li>Threaded End</li><li>Welding End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nozzle check valves market comprises three main types based on their end connection methods. Flanged end valves feature flat circular flanges for bolting to connecting pipes, ensuring a secure and leak-proof fit, ideal for high-pressure applications. Threaded end valves utilize male and female threads for easy installation and disassembly, suitable for smaller pipelines. Welding end valves have smooth ends designed for direct welding to pipes, providing a robust and permanent connection, commonly used in high-strength and high-temperature environments.</p></p>
