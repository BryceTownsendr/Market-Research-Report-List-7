<p><p>The Raman Spectroscopy Technology market encompasses various techniques to enhance signal detection. Surface-enhanced Raman scattering (SERS) amplifies the Raman signal using metallic surfaces, improving sensitivity for trace analysis. Tip-enhanced Raman scattering (TERS) combines SERS with scanning probe microscopy for nanoscale imaging, allowing for detailed material characterization. Additionally, the "Others" segment includes emerging techniques and hybrid methods, broadening the application range in fields like biomedicine, materials science, and environmental monitoring, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
