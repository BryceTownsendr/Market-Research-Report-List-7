<p><p>The polyurethane rubber market is primarily divided into two types: solid systems and liquid systems. Solid systems are pre-formulated, offering durability and resistance suitable for applications like automotive parts and industrial components. Liquid systems, on the other hand, are versatile and can be molded to specific shapes, ideal for coatings, adhesives, and casting applications. Both types provide unique properties such as elasticity and chemical resistance, catering to diverse industries including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
