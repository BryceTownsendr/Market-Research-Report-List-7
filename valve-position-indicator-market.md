<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The valve position indicator market is segmented into two main types: single acting positioners and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners control valves using air pressure in one direction, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, often used for smaller applications. In contrast, double acting positioners use air pressure in both directions, providing more precise control and faster response times, making them suitable for larger and more complex systems. Both types are essential for optimizing valve performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
