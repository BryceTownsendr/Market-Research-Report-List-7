<p><p>The e-bike drive system market consists of three main types: front-drive, mid-drive, and rear-drive systems. Front-drive systems place the motor at the front wheel, offering good traction but limited balance. Mid-drive systems mount the motor near the bike's pedals, providing a more balanced weight distribution and better efficiency on varied terrain. Rear-drive systems are located at the back wheel, delivering strong acceleration and a natural riding feel. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences.</p></p>
