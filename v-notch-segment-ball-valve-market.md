Flanged valves feature flanges on both ends, allowing easy connection to piping systems, which is beneficial for high-pressure applications. In contrast, flangeless valves, also known as weld-end valves, are designed for direct welding to pipes, providing a more streamlined installation. Both types serve specific operational needs in fluid control, catering to various industrial sectors such as oil and gas, water treatment, and process manufacturing.</p></p>
