<p><p>Rotary injection molding machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operation. Vertical machines feature a vertically oriented injection unit and mold, allowing for efficient use of space and easy access for loading and unloading parts. They are ideal for small, precise components. In contrast, horizontal machines have a horizontally aligned system, facilitating larger production runs and handling complex shapes with greater versatility. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying industry needs.</p></p>
