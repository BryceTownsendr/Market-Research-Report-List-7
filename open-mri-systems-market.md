<p><ul><li>Low Field Strength</li><li>Medium Field Strength</li><li>High Field Strength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open MRI systems are categorized into three market types based on their magnetic field strength. Low field strength systems typically operate below 1.0 Tesla, offering less detailed images but greater patient comfort and accessibility. Medium field strength systems range from 1.0 to 1.5 Tesla, providing a balance of image quality and patient experience. High field strength systems, above 1.5 Tesla, deliver superior image clarity and resolution but may be less accommodating for claustrophobic patients. Each type serves different clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
