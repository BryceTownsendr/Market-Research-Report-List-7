<p><strong>The Thermally Conductive Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Conductive Additives</li><li>Thermosetting Conductive Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermally conductive additives market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic conductive additives and thermosetting conductive additives. Thermoplastic conductive additives are incorporated into polymers that can be reprocessed, offering flexibility and easy manufacturing. In contrast, thermosetting conductive additives are used in materials that undergo a chemical change to achieve a solid form, providing enhanced durability and thermal resistance. Both types improve heat transfer in applications like electronics, automotive, and aerospace, facilitating efficient thermal management.</p></p>
