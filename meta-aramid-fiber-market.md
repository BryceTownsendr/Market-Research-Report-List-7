<p><p>Meta-aramid fibers, primarily encompassing PPTA (Polyparaphenylene Terephthalamide) and PMIA (Poly(m-Phenylene Isophthalamide)), are known for their exceptional thermal stability and resistance to chemical degradation. PPTA is widely used in applications requiring high strength and flame resistance, such as aerospace and protective clothing. PMIA, recognized for its excellent dielectric properties, is utilized in electrical insulation and high-performance applications. Both fibers contribute to industries like automotive, firefighting, and military, due to their lightweight and durable nature, promoting safety and performance.</p></p>
