<p><p>The veterinary teleradiology market encompasses various imaging modalities utilized for diagnosing animal health. X-rays provide quick, conventional imaging for bone injuries and diseases. Computerized Tomography (CT) scans offer detailed cross-sectional images, aiding in complex diagnostics. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) delivers high-resolution images suitable for soft tissue evaluation. Ultrasound scans are used for real-time imaging of internal organs, while nuclear scans help visualize metabolic processes and detect abnormalities. These technologies enhance veterinary care through remote access and expert analysis.</p></p>
