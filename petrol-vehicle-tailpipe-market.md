<p><p>The petrol vehicle tailpipe market includes two main types: single tailpipe and double tailpipes. Single tailpipe systems are simpler, cost-effective, and commonly found in standard or compact vehicles, offering basic exhaust functionality. Double tailpipes, often seen in performance or luxury cars, enhance aesthetics, improve exhaust flow, and reduce backpressure, boosting engine efficiency and power. Both types cater to different vehicle segments, with single tailpipes focusing on practicality and double tailpipes emphasizing performance and design appeal.</p></p>
