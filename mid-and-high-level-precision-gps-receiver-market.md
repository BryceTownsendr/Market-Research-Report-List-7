<p><p>The Mid-and High-Level Precision GPS Receiver market comprises primarily two types: Single Frequency and Dual Frequency receivers. Single Frequency GPS receivers operate on a single frequency band, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic applications. In contrast, Dual Frequency GPS receivers utilize two frequency bands, enabling them to mitigate errors caused by atmospheric conditions and improve positioning accuracy. As a result, Dual Frequency receivers are preferred for applications requiring higher precision, such as surveying, agriculture, and autonomous navigation.</p></p>
